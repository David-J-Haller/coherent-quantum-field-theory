# Research Context

## What is CQT?

The **Coherent Quantum Field Theory (CQT)** is a theoretical research framework investigating whether coherent quantum field dynamics may play a role in biological and consciousness-related processes.

The project combines concepts from:

- quantum field theory
- open quantum systems
- nonlinear dynamics
- biological coherence research
- consciousness studies

CQT is presented as an interdisciplinary theoretical program rather than as an established consensus model.

## Numerical Research Context

The simulation framework associated with CQT explores nonlinear mean-field dynamics in coherent open quantum systems with biologically motivated coupling structures.

The simulations are intended to document the numerical architecture of the theory and to support reproducibility and future extensions.

---

## Current Research Position

CQT should be understood as an ongoing independent research program.

This repository does not claim peer-reviewed validation by itself.
Instead, it provides a structured public interface for:

- documentation
- citation
