# Abstract

The Coherent Quantum Field Theory (CQT) proposes a theoretical framework
in which biological systems interact with coherent quantum fields through
nonlinear open-system dynamics.

The theory extends conventional mean-field models of driven dissipative
quantum systems by introducing biologically motivated coupling mechanisms
and memory feedback processes.

Numerical simulations demonstrate regimes of multistability and attractor
formation, suggesting that coherent field dynamics may play a role in
biological information processing.

The work connects concepts from:

- quantum field theory
- open quantum systems
- nonlinear dynamical systems
- biological coherence phenomena

CQT is presented as a theoretical research program exploring the possible
role of coherent quantum fields in biological and cognitive processes.
