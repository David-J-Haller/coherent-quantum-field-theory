# Coherent Quantum Field Theory (CQT)

**Author:** David J. Haller

A theoretical framework exploring the interaction of coherent quantum fields with biological systems and consciousness.

---

## Overview

The **Coherent Quantum Field Theory (CQT)** proposes that biological systems may interact with coherent quantum fields through nonlinear open-system dynamics.

The framework extends conventional mean-field descriptions of driven dissipative quantum systems by introducing biologically motivated coupling mechanisms and memory feedback processes.

Numerical simulations demonstrate regimes of multistability and attractor formation within the model.

The theory connects concepts from:

- quantum field theory
- open quantum systems
- nonlinear dynamical systems
- biological coherence phenomena
- consciousness research

CQT is presented as an interdisciplinary research program exploring the potential role of coherent quantum fields in biological and cognitive processes.
